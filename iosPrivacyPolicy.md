# Privacy Policy for Onshape Plus

Last Updated: June 2026

Onshape Plus is a Safari extension and companion application designed to enhance the Onshape user experience on iPad.

## Information We Collect

Onshape Plus does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The application does not:

* Require user accounts
* Track user activity
* Collect analytics
* Store personal information
* Use advertising identifiers
* Sell or share user data

## Data Processing

All functionality provided by Onshape Plus runs locally on the user's device and within the user's browser.

## Third-Party Services

Onshape Plus does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Users may choose to access third-party websites, including Onshape and GitHub, through links provided by the application. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Onshape Plus does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted at this location with a revised "Last Updated" date.
