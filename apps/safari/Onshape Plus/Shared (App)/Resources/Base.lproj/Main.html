Onshape Plus

Make Onshape faster, cleaner, and easier to use on iPad.

Enable the Safari Extension

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tab Apps on the left sidebar
  3. Go to Safari
  4. Tap Extensions
  5. Tap Onshape Plus
  6. Toggle on Allow Extension
  7. Change Permissions from Ask to Allow
  8. Open Onshape in Safari
